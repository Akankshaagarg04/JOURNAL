FAMOUS JOURNALS

1. Nature Genetics



Nature Genetics publishes the articles related to the genetic basis for common and complex diseases and on the functional mechanism, architecture, and evolution of gene networks. Topics of studies include -Genes in the pathology of human disease -Molecular analysis of simple and complex genetic traits -Cancer genetics -Agricultural genomics -Developmental genetics

2. Nature Medicine



Nature Medicine publishes research that includes areas of interest -Gene and cell therapies -Clinical genomics -Regenerative medicine -High-definition medicine -Effects of the environment in human health -Artificial intelligence in health care -Smart wearable devices -Early disease diagnosis -Microbiome -Aging.

3. Nature Materials



Nature Materials is a monthly multi-disciplinary journal that covers areas of study like- Chemistry, Mechanical Engineering, mechanics of materials, material science, physics, astronomy, and condensed matter physics.
